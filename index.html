Our Launch Strategy

We analyzed key urban areas across India to identify cities ready for smart e-scooter adoption.

  • Chennai High student & IT mobility demand
  • Hyderabad Tech hubs & wide metro gaps
  • Pune Eco-conscious youth commuters
  • 2.3x Growth In micro-mobility sector (2024)
  • 92% Want fast EV transport

We’re focused on Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities with dense college clusters, IT parks, and last-mile gaps. Early research shows a sharp rise in demand for low-cost, green transport. Zoro Mobility fills that gap with instant, app-powered access.

How It Works

Get riding in three simple steps.

1. Download the App
